Who Am I

Hello, I'm Evanson Igiri, a software developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I'm a critical Thinker, analytical and very passionate about software development. Through my time in software development I acquired professional-level skills including HTML, CSS, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React, Redux, Node, Sass, Test-Driven development techniques and more. I designed and develped over 40 projects using remote peer programming with other software developers(coding partners) around the world thereby building my teamwork and communication skills.

I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive interface designs.

  • Strong preference for easy to use, intuitive UX/UI.
  • Websites don't have to be static, I love making pages come to life.
  • My layouts will work on any device, big or small.
  • Fast load times and lag free interaction, my highest priority.
